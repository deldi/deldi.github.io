About Me

Hi! I'm Deldi.

I am an independent singer-songwriter based in the greater Seattle area.

I like to think of the songs I write as paper airplanes. As scary as it can be to chuck my lyric sheets out my window for the world to consume, I believe that the risk of laying bare my vulnerabilities is well worth it if my paper airplanes happen to land in the hands of someone who really needed them—someone who I couldn't have reached otherwise.

With all that said, I hope my music uplifts you. I hope it helps you heal.
